Built-In Demand

Your students already
need this gear.

Academy gear isn't like retail clothing. You already have customers walking through your door every day. These demand triggers make sell-through far more predictable than most people expect.

🥋

New Students

Every new student needs a gi. If you stock your brand, they buy your brand — not a random one from Amazon.

🏆

Tournaments

Competition season creates strong demand for team-branded gear. Students want to represent on the mat.

🎽

Belt Promotions

Earning a new belt is a milestone. A fresh gi for a fresh rank is a natural, high-emotion purchase trigger.

📅

Seasonal Drops

Limited drops create urgency and collector behavior. Students who miss one look forward to the next.

🔄

Replacement Purchases

High-volume practitioners who train daily wear out gear fast. Your most dedicated students are repeat buyers.

🌟

Instructor Influence

What you wear on the mat, your students notice. Instructor adoption drives team-wide adoption naturally.

The Process

From application
to delivered gear —
step by step.

We designed this for academy owners running a gym, not managing a supply chain. Your time investment is minimal. Ours is significant. That's the deal.

01
Qualification Call

We start with a quick call to learn about your academy — size, goals, current gear setup, timeline. We're selective about who we work with, and we'll tell you honestly if the timing isn't right. If you're a fit, we move fast.

~30 min
02
Design Session

You send us your logo and a few design references. Our team builds the full tech pack — colorways, placements, construction specs. You approve the design. We handle all factory communication. You don't need to know anything about manufacturing.

2–3 weeks
03
Pre-Order Campaign

Before anything goes into production, we help you run a pre-order with your community. We provide presale flyer templates, social media assets, and launch strategy. This tells you exactly how many to order — and often funds the purchase before you spend a dollar.

Optional but powerful
04
Production

Once you approve the purchase order and submit your deposit, production begins. We track your order with the factory and keep you updated throughout. Standard lead time is 90–120 days. Nothing ships without your final sign-off.

90–120 days
05
Launch & Sell-Through Support

When your gear arrives, we don't disappear. We help you set up your Shopify store, run email campaigns, and plan your drop launch. We track sell-through rate together — and when you hit 70%+, we start planning your next drop.

Ongoing
06
Repeat & Scale

After 2–3 drops, most academies shift from "running a gear order" to "operating an apparel program." That means predictable non-tuition revenue, stronger brand identity, and a reason for students to stay engaged between sessions.
